THE FIRST TRIAL IS MOVEMENT
GATE I

AIR

The first element you shall learn is Air.

It cannot be grasped, yet it surrounds you.

It bends without breaking.
It moves without being seen.
It shapes the world without leaving a mark.

Air does not answer force with force.

It answers action with reaction.

To command it, you must understand one truth:

Control is not domination.
It is awareness.
