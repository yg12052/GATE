The Book Has Opened

To the one who has found this book:

Know that you were not led here by chance.

If your eyes now trace these lines,
then you have been chosen for a purpose few have carried since the dawn of magic.

Four gates exist within this world —
and their power is such that their very existence was hidden from history, from record,
and from the memory of mortals and sorcerers alike.

I am the last who knew their resting places.
And so I concealed them —
lest they fall into the hands of those whose hearts bend toward ruin and hunger for dominion.

Understand this:

The path before you is neither simple nor safe.
Yet if you have reached this page, it means that within you dwells a spirit capable of bearing the burden of the Four Elements:

Air.
Water.
Earth.
Fire.

Each gate is bound not only by riddle,
but by the nature of the element it guards.
You will not pass by intellect alone.
You must listen.
Observe.
Adapt.

In the moment you are ready to know more,
the book shall reveal another page.

You cannot force it forward.
You cannot hold it back.

It will move in accordance with your progress.

And when the final seal trembles —
you will understand why this knowledge was buried.

Proceed with care.
The elements remember.
